Work

My graduate work at the Simon Fraser University Department of Chemistry has focused on studies of nuclear structure, detection systems, and radiation safety.

What am I working on right now?

I'm studying excited states in 28Mg (a neutron-rich radioactive isotope of magnesium) at high energy, some of which have not been seen before. Also working on my PhD thesis, ETA spring 2019.
